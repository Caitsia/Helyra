Distance: (0.7 AU) 1st from it's Star Classification: magma planet

About Pyronis

Terrestrial magma planet with extreme volcanic activity and a molten surface. design and lore inspired by venus's surface and the volcanic activity observed there. in addition to magma exoplanets observed in other star systems, such as k2-141b. Pyronis contains massive oceans of lava and a perpetually changing landscape due to volcanic eruptions, shifting tectonic plates, and intense heat. The planet's atmosphere is thick with volcanic gases, creating a harsh environment that challenges any form of life as we know it.
